Our Mission

GHUMAKKAD is more than just a travel booking site. We aim to take trekkers and campers to underrated & spiritual locations in India while also giving back to local communities.

Why We Exist?

Many beautiful trekking spots remain unexplored. Our platform helps connect like-minded travelers and offers a chance to **teach and donate** to locals.

Impact Through Data

Our analysis shows that 70% of travelers prefer immersive experiences over luxury travel. By adding a community-helping aspect, we bring a meaningful impact.
